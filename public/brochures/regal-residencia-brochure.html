1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

Regal Residencia is a premium residential development located in the heart of Mohali, Punjab. Our project represents the pinnacle of modern luxury living with world-class amenities and thoughtfully designed spaces for contemporary families.

Key Features:

  • Prime Location in Sector 70, Mohali
  • 3BHK and 4BHK Ultra-Luxury Apartments
  • Modern Contemporary Architecture
  • Premium German & Italian Sanitaryware
  • Vitrified Tiles & Designer Wallpapers
  • Green Building Concepts
  • 24/7 Security with CCTV Surveillance

2. LOCATION ADVANTAGES

Strategically located in Sector 70, Mohali, Regal Residencia offers excellent connectivity:

  • 5 minutes from Chandigarh Airport
  • 10 minutes from Chandigarh IT Park
  • 15 minutes from Mohali Railway Station
  • Close to leading schools, hospitals, and shopping centers
  • Easy access to major highways

3. UNIT TYPES & SPECIFICATIONS

3BHK Luxury Apartments (1450 sq.ft.)

  • Spacious living area with open-concept dining
  • Master bedroom with ensuite bathroom and walk-in closet
  • Servant quarters with separate entry
  • Modular kitchen with breakfast counter
  • Private balcony with city views

3BHK Premium Apartments (1565 sq.ft.)

  • Expansive living room with double-height ceiling
  • Luxurious master suite with private study
  • Premium modular kitchen with island counter

  • Two balconies for enhanced ventilation

4BHK Grand Apartments (2190 sq.ft.)

  • Grand living area with entertainment zone
  • Private study room for work from home
  • Wine cellar for connoisseurs
  • Four bedrooms with individual ensuites
  • Private elevator access for penthouse units
