1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

Regal Residencia is a premium residential development located in the heart of Mohali, Punjab. Our project represents the pinnacle of modern luxury living with world-class amenities and thoughtfully designed spaces for contemporary families.

Key Features:
- Prime Location in Sector 70, Mohali
- 3BHK and 4BHK Ultra-Luxury Apartments
- Modern Contemporary Architecture
- Premium German & Italian Sanitaryware
- Vitrified Tiles & Designer Wallpapers
- Green Building Concepts
- 24/7 Security with CCTV Surveillance
